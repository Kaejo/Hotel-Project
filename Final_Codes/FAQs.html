ROOM FACILITY



Our standard check-in time is 2pm and check-out time is 12 noon.



An early check-in is subjected to availability. To secure an early check-in, we recommend customers to book the room one night in advance. Should your room is not ready for check-in, we will register you first and assist you to store your luggage. You will be given a temporary pass to access and visit the various facilities in the hotel until your room is ready.



Late check-out is available. However, a half-day room charge may be incurred for a late check-out between 12pm to 6pm. A full-day room charge will apply for a later check-out after 6pm.



Yes, you can request for an extra bed. An additional charges will apply. Should you need any assistance, feel free to contact us at +65 6060 7071
(between 9am to 9pm daily, Singapore Timing), or email us at enquries@grandoceaniaharbourfront.com.



Yes, you may store your luggage before check-in or after check-out with our Bell Desk at the hotel lobby, Level 1. Alternatively, you may wish to contact our Direct Assistance Team (Express Service) by using the phone in the hotel room to assist with your luggage.



OTHER FACILITIES



The fitness centre opens at 7am and closes at 10.30pm.



The SPA operates from 9am to 10.30pm.



The indoor/outdoor swimming pool operates from 5am to 11pm. The use of swimming pool facility after 11pm is strictly not allowed.
