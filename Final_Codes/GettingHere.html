Getting Here

Nearest MRT :

Harbourfont Station NE1/CC29

Bus :

10, 30, 30e, 57, 61, 65, 80, 97, 97e, 100,
123M, 131, 143, 145, 166, 855, NR1, NR6

From MRT:

Checkpoints to clear to reach the hotel

  1. From Harbourfont NE1/CC29 take Exit D.
  2. Walk straight along the sheltered walkway until you see the first traffic light junction.
  3. At the traffic light junction, turn left, walk straight up and you will reach Grand Oceania Harbourfront.

From Bus Stops:

Checkpoints to clear to reach the hotel

  1. From bus station Harbourfront Stn/Vivocity (14141).
    1. Upon alighting at Harbourfront Stn/Vivocity (14141) bus stop, turn left and walk straight.
    2. Walk straight till a traffic light is visible.
    3. Cross the traffic light and the following one on the left.
    4. Walk straight and you will reach Grand Oceania Harbourfront.

  2. From bus station Opp VivoCity (14119).
    1. Upon alighting at Opp VivoCity (14119) bus stop, turn right and walk straight.
    2. Walk along the sheltered walkway until you see the first traffic light junction.
    3. At the traffic light junction you see, turn left, walk straight up and you will reach Grand Oceania Harbourfront.
